## Assembly Guide

**Total clips:** 34
**Total runtime:** 272 seconds (4 minutes 32 seconds)

**Recommended assembly order:**
1. Intro (Clip 1)
2. Console intro then its agents, repeated for all 7 consoles (Clips 2-32)
3. AI Operatives Hub (Clip 33)
4. Finale (Clip 34)

**Transition suggestion:** Use 0.5s cross-dissolve between clips within the same console, and 1s wipe/glitch transition between console sections.

**Audio suggestion:** Layer a driving electronic/cinematic soundtrack across the full edit. Add subtle whoosh sound effects on transitions and UI animation sounds on data visualizations.

**Color coding per console for visual consistency:**
- Customer Portal: Blue
- Field Operations: Green
- Business Operations: Purple
- Outreach and Sales: Orange
- Social Media: Pink/Magenta
- Creative and Web Presence: Violet
- Analytics and Reports: Cyan/Teal
- AI Operatives Hub: Indigo
- Intro/Finale: Deep Blue + Gold
