---
name: 90-Day Live Trial Standard
description: 90-day trial, onboarding fee due at start, progress math, reminders
type: feature
---
- All plans include a 90-Day Live Trial. First 30 days = Concierge Onboarding, then 60 days of full live use.
- One-time onboarding fee is invoiced at the START of the trial (non-refundable once onboarding completes).
- Progress bar math: (90 - daysRemaining) / 90.
- Reminder emails at 7d, 3d, 1d remaining (see trial-reminders edge function).
- Customer-facing copy must say "90-Day Live Trial" consistently across UI, TOS, intake, marketing.
